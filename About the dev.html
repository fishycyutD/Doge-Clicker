Doge Clicker

Doge Clicker is a clicker game involving a Doge who was
delivered in a mysterious package that appeared out of no
where(as seen in the trailer).
... who was it from, how did it get there? was it the one i saw...
Whatever, moving on, you don't know what to do with him,
so you decide to take care of it. The Doge for some reason
has seemingly more intense feelings than other dogs, even
really clingy ones, when left alone. You make money
(specifically DogeCoin) from petting the Doge which you then
use to buy stuff, and eventually travel to the moon, then other places.
